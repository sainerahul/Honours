Atirikt

Atirikt which means extra or surplus in Hindi is a free food sharing app.
Anyone from a shop owner to a housewife can use it and the idea is that instead of throwing away perfectly edible food, you can give it away to someone else who won’t let it go to waste.
This will not only reduce binning of food but will also help feed a person in need.

Our History

Started in 2010, Atirikt has quickly established itself as a force in reducing food wastage and promoting food sharing culture, Atirik is totally free of cost and helps food providers to link with person in need us.
